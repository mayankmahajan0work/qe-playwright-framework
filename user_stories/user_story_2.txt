As a Sales Representative,
I want to convert a qualified lead into an account, contact, and opportunity,
so that I can begin the sales process for a real customer.

Acceptance Criteria:

Scenario 1:Convert Lead
Given a Lead is marked as "Qualified"
When I convert the Lead
Then a new Account should be created
And a new Contact should be created
And a new Opportunity should be created
And the Lead should be marked as Converted

Scenario 2:Verify converted records
Given a Lead has been converted
When I navigate to the Account record
Then the Contact and Opportunity created from the Lead should be associated with the Account
