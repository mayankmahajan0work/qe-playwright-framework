As a Sales Representative,
I want to manage opportunities through different sales stages,
so that I can track progress and close deals successfully.

Acceptance Criteria:

Scenario 1: Create Opportunity from Lead
Given a Lead is converted
Then an Opportunity should be created with stage "Prospecting"

Scenario 2: Progress Opportunity stages
Given an Opportunity exists
When I update the Opportunity stage sequentially
Then the stage and probability should update correctly

Scenario 3: Close Opportunity
Given an Opportunity is in Proposal stage
When I update the stage to "Closed Won"
Then the Opportunity should be marked as Closed Won
